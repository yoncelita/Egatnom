We install steel structures,
platforms and storage racks

We founded our company in 2021 with one mission only - to put our years-long expertise in assembling steel structures and storage racks to good use.
To create value.
To facilitate and assist vital processes.
To build trusted and lasting partnerships with clients.

We are dreamers, yes.
But even more than that - we are doers.

What we do is deliver and assemble steel racks in storage facilities and production halls, taking care of the entire transportation process, as well as the assembly documentation needed. Our product portfolio includes pallet racking systems, automated storage and retrieval systems, AKL racks, APL racks, roller conveyors, steel structures, floorings (both wooden and slatted), Commissioners, and Mezzanine floorings.

We handle, monitor and navigate the whole process:  from the initial measurement of structures to the protocol handover on the site.

Our vision

Making a positive impact and facilitating the process of steel structure assembly - by providing high-quality services and products. By being transparent, reliable and loyal in all business transactions with our clients.

Our mission

Building and developing trusted, satisfactory and long-lasting business relations with our clients. To make that possible, we provide and ensure premium quality services, as our teams diligently adhere to the deadlines set.

Our values

We put an emphasis on doing our job perfectly. We're team players and sticklers for high-quality performance, satisfactory collaboration, and mutual respect.

What makes us different?

We believe that in order to excel in the industry and continuously develop our expertise, we need to work with high quality equipment, provide exceptional service, meet deadlines and always be reliable and trustworthy in our relations with our clients.

That is why handle, monitor and navigate the whole process: from the initial measurement of structures to the protocol handover on the site. When working with our clients, we provide the equipment, the measuring instruments and the experienced team that performs the assembly. We take care of the transport by forklift, of the delivery, of the assembly, and of the site cleaning post completion.

Because to us, the most valuable success metric, is client satisfaction.

Our services

We are making the project from A to Z.

Documentation

Preparation and handling of the assembly documentation, in adherence to the laws applicable in our client's country.

Measuring

Complete site and equipment measurement based on drawings by Hilti Lasers PR30-HVS, also optic measuring equipment by Hilti.

Forklift transport

Forklift delivery of the steel structure equipment to the site of the client.

Welding

Performing and overseeing the entire steel structure welding process.

Assembly

Complete assembly of the steel construction, in accordance with safety, functionality and quality guidelines.

Cleaning

Handling all the cleaning procedures necessary after delivery, assembly and completion.

Final protocol

Providing and concluding the handover protocol on the site of the client.

Assistance

Assistance on site takeover.
